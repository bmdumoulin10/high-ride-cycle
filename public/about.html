About

Located in the SloHi neighborhood of Denver, CO, High Ride incorporates cardio, weights, and resistance to provide a full body workout. A combination of music, lighting, and energy help create a positive and rewarding experience for each one of our riders. It's a party on a bike! Our experienced and motivated instructors will guide you through each energizing 45 minute workout. Come get your sweat on and take the High Ride!
