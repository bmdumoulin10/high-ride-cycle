FAQ

Ride With Us

Our bikes fill up fast so be sure to book a bike early. Sign up for classes by clicking the Ride With Me button above. You can sign up for class a week in advance. Cancellations will be accepted up to 1 hour before a class by canceling your reservation in your account. A late cancel or no-show will result in a $10 fee.

How much does it cost?

To see our pricing, go to our class price list or our monthly membership price list. You can buy a single class, a package of classes for a discounted price or a monthly unlimited pass. If you buy class packs, those credits never expire. Monthly unlimited passes automatically renew each month or you can buy them on a one-off basis. Everything can be purchased online or in the studio.

How early should I get to class?

Arrive 15 minutes before class starts to get checked in, pick your bike, and get your bike fitted to you! You must check in no later than 5 minutes prior to the scheduled class start time or your bike will be released to the waitlist. No-shows will result in a $10 fee and be released to the waitlist.

How do I set up my bike?

Whether you’re new to indoor cycling or just new to our bikes, your instructor can help you adjust your bike to fit your body for the safest, most comfortable, and efficient ride.

Are cycling shoes required?

Yup! We’re clipping in. Don’t you worry though, we provide cycling shoes to all riders free of charge. Our bikes are compatible with both LOOK Delta and SPD clips.

Do you have lockers available?

We have plenty of cubbies for your use to stow your stuff during class and while you change. Please note we are not responsible for any lost or stolen items.

What should I wear?

Come dressed for a sweaty workout! We recommend wearing fitted moisture wicking clothing.

What are classes like?

Cycling is a fast pace, high intensity workout geared towards all levels. You will get a full body workout with amazing moves, funky tunes, and psychedelic lights. It’s a party on a bike!

Do you have showers?

Yes! We have two showers available for use fully equipped with towels, shower supplies, and blow dryers.
